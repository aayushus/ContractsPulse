PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of May 20, 2026 (“Effective Date”) by and between:

Customer: Acme Manufacturing, Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Customer”)
Vendor: Brightline Consulting LLC, a New York limited liability company (“Vendor”)

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Vendor will provide the services described in Exhibit A (“Services”). Customer may request additional services by written change order.

2. TERM
The initial term of this Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for twelve (12) months (the “Initial Term”), unless earlier terminated as provided herein. This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT
Customer will pay Vendor the fees set forth in Exhibit B. All invoices are due net fifteen (15) days from invoice date. Past due amounts accrue interest at 1.5% per month (or the maximum allowed by law, if lower).

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party may receive Confidential Information of the other. The receiving party will protect Confidential Information using at least reasonable care and will not disclose it to any third party except as required to perform under this Agreement. Confidentiality obligations will continue for three (3) years after termination.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product, inventions, discoveries, developments, processes, documentation, and materials created, conceived, or reduced to practice by Vendor (alone or with others) during the term of this Agreement, whether or not related to the Services (collectively, “Work Product”), shall be the exclusive property of Customer. Vendor hereby irrevocably assigns to Customer all right, title, and interest in and to the Work Product.

6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
EXCEPT FOR A PARTY’S GROSS NEGLIGENCE OR WILLFUL MISCONDUCT, IN NO EVENT WILL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES. CUSTOMER’S TOTAL LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT WILL NOT EXCEED THE AMOUNTS PAID BY CUSTOMER UNDER THIS AGREEMENT IN THE THREE (3) MONTHS PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO THE CLAIM.

7. INDEMNIFICATION
Vendor will indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Customer from and against any third-party claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys’ fees) arising out of or relating to (a) Vendor’s breach of this Agreement, (b) Vendor’s negligence or willful misconduct, or (c) any allegation that the Services or deliverables infringe any intellectual property right. Vendor’s indemnification obligations are uncapped.

8. TERMINATION
Customer may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ten (10) days’ prior written notice. Vendor may terminate only for Customer’s material breach that remains uncured for thirty (30) days following written notice.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement will be governed by the laws of the State of New York, without regard to conflict of laws rules. Any disputes will be resolved exclusively in the state or federal courts located in New York County, New York.

Description of Services: Vendor will provide procurement analytics consulting and contract review support.

EXHIBIT B
